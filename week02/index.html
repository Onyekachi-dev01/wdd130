Nigeria

Nigeria, the "Giant of Africa," is a West African nation renowned as the continent's most populous country (over 230 million) and a major economic power.Gaining independence in 1960, it is a diverse Federal Republic of 36 states with over 250 ethnic groups (primarily Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo).
Abuja is the capital, while Lagos is the primary economic hub.
